Banana Split Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup milk, divided into three
  • 1 scoop Selecta Super Thick Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 3 pcs Ripe Banana
  • 1 scoop Selecta Super Thick Chocolate Ice Cream
  • 1 scoop Selecta Super Thick Strawberry Ice Cream
  • 2 swirls Whipped Cream (Optional)
  • Candy Sprinkles
  • Cherry (Optional)

Procedure:

  1. In a blender, combine Â¼ cup milk, Selecta Super Thick Vanilla Ice Cream, and 1 banana. Blend until smooth, then set aside.
  2. Repeat step 1 for Selecta Super Thick Chocolate Ice Cream and Selecta Thick Strawberry Ice Cream.
  3. Assemble your milkshake: Layer the flavors in a tall glass. Garnish with whipped cream, candy sprinkles, and a cherry. Serve immediately.
