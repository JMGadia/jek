Kinilaw na Isda

Ingredients:

  • 500 g fresh tanigue, sliced into ¾-inch-thick cubes
  • 2 cups cane vinegar or coconut vinegar, divided
  • 1 pc red onion, minced
  • 1 pc ginger, cut into 2-inch-thick strips
  • bird’s eye chilis, chopped
  • fresh long green chilis, chopped
  • Knorr Sinigang Sa Sampalok Mix

Procedure:

  1. Combine tanigue and 1 cup vinegar in a bowl. Mix well, then discard any excess liquid.
  2. Mix the remaining vinegar, onion, ginger, and chilies in another bowl. Add tanigue 3-5 minutes before serving.
  3. Season with Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix. Adjust spices to taste. Serve immediately.
