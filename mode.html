S'more A La Mode

Ingredients:

  • 2 scoop selecta super thick classic vanilla ice cream
  • 8 brownies cubed
  • 1/4 graham crackers crushed
  • marshmallows
  • chocolate syrup

Procedure:

  1. Scoop Selecta Super Thick Classic Vanilla ice cream into a glass.
  2. Add the brownie cubes, then the crushed grahams, before adding another scoop of ice cream.
  3. To prepare the mallows, place 1.2 cup of mini mallows in a bake-safe pan and bake at 190 degrees Celsius for 10 minutes, or use a culinary torch to brown the mallows. Use the mallows as topping for the sundae.
  4. Drizzle the s'mores sundae with chocolate syrup to serve.
