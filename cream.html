Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 3-5 scoops Selecta Super Thick Classic Vanilla
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup milk
  • Whipped cream, optional
  • Cookie crumbs, optional

Procedure:

  1. To make the syrup, the brown sugar and water in a saucepan under low heat. Bring to a simmer and stir until all the sugar has been dissolved.
  2. In a blender, mix Selecta Super Thick Classic Vanilla, milk, and bananas. Blend until fully combined and smooth.
  3. To serve, drizzle the brown sugar syrup on the inside of a tall glass. Pour in the milkshake mixture to fill. For optional garnish, top with whipped cream and cookie crumbs to serve.
